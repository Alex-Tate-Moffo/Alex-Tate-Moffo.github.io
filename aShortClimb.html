A Short Climb

Created by Thomas Marshall and Alex Tate-Moffo, A Short Climb is a short vertical platformer made in Unity. The game was a final project for CITA280: Game Programming in Spring 2021.

Race to the top of a tree to get back your locket, stolen by a terrifying owl while you were sleeping! Along the way, jump between platforms, dodge enemies, and collect nuts - if you collect them all, maybe you will have enough strength to steal the locket back?
