A Short Climb

Created by Thomas Marshall and Alex Tate-Moffo, A Short Climb is a short vertical platformer made in Unity. The game was a final project for CITA280: Game Programming in Spring 2021.

Race to the top of a tree to get back your locket, stolen by a terrifying owl while you were sleeping! Along the way, jump between platforms, dodge enemies, and collect nuts - if you collect them all, maybe you will have enough strength to steal the locket back?

Development Process

1) Pitch

I pitched the idea for the game, which overall remained unchanged throughout the design process.

2) Level Prototype

Thomas joined me on the project, and together we created a theoretical layout for the main level as our "paper" prototype.

3) Gameplay Prototype

I worked collaboratively with Thomas to program A Short Climb. The game went through a few cycles of development, starting out as a very simplistic gameplay demo with no art or music assets.

4) Finished Documentation

We completed a Game Design Document for the project detailing all of the finalized elements and crediting.
